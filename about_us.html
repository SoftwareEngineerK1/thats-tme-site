Our Story

T.M.E (The Melanin Experience) is more than an event series—it is a curated collective designed to bridge the gap between digital discovery and real-world connection.

THE VISION

To create a sanctuary of curated experiences that celebrate culture, foster high-level networking, and provide exclusive access to the best the city has to offer.

From Digital to Real-Life

Born from the community at BMOREFoodies, T.M.E serves as the physical manifestation of our digital culture. We take the hidden gems, the elite vibes, and the most sought-after moments and bring them to life for our "Insider Network."

Whether it’s an intimate dinner, a high-energy festival, or a professional development gala, every T.M.E moment is TailorMade for you.
